🌱 EcoScan

Make Conscious Choices. Protect the Planet.

Scan. Learn. Choose Better.

EcoScan is an AI-powered web application designed to help consumers make more environmentally conscious purchasing decisions. By scanning product barcodes or packaging, you receive instant feedback on the item's environmental impact.

Empowering responsible consumption through technology and data transparency.

⚡ How It Works

1

Scan a Barcode

Use your camera to scan any product barcode or packaging using our mobile or web scanner.

2

Get Instant Insights

Receive detailed environmental impact data including carbon footprint and sustainability scores powered by peer-reviewed environmental databases.

3

Discover Alternatives

Explore eco-friendly alternatives and make informed purchasing decisions that align with your values.

4

Track & Compare

Compare products, track your sustainability goals, and engage with community-driven feedback.

✨ Key Features

📊 Environmental Impact Analysis

Get comprehensive data on carbon footprint, supply chain sustainability, and packaging impact using machine learning analysis of environmental databases.

🎯 Sustainability Scoring

Products receive eco-friendly rates that help you quickly understand their environmental impact at a glance.

💡 Smart Alternatives

Discover eco-friendly alternatives to your favorite products with detailed comparison insights.

🎨 Beautiful Interface

Simple, user-friendly design makes scanning and exploring product impacts effortless and intuitive.

🚀 Personalized Goals

Set and track your sustainability goals, watching your positive impact grow over time.

👥 Community Feedback

Join a community of conscious consumers sharing insights and driving accountability for brands.

🌍 About EcoScan

EcoScan leverages advanced machine learning to analyse data from publicly available environmental databases, supply chain information, and product metadata. Our mission is to raise awareness and promote responsible consumption through technology and data transparency.

Every purchase has an environmental impact. EcoScan empowers you to understand that impact and make choices that contribute to a more sustainable future.
